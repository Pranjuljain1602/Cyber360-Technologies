Identity and Access management

"Identity and Access Management (IAM) in cybersecurity refers to the framework of policies, technologies, and processes that organizations use to ensure that the right individuals have the appropriate access to resources, systems, and data. The primary goal of IAM is to maintain the security and integrity of an organization's information while managing and controlling access effectively."

"IAM Essentials: Safeguarding Cybersecurity Access"

Identity and Access Management (IAM) in cybersecurity is crucial for ensuring secure and authorized access to systems and data. It involves managing user identities, enforcing access policies, and safeguarding sensitive information, playing a pivotal role in overall cybersecurity resilience.
